How It Works

Wind down. Drift off. Wake up smarter.

Four seamless steps. One app handles everything from your bedtime routine to your morning intelligence report.

01
Wind Down
Choose a sleep story, meditation, or soundscape. MyDriftLAB builds your personalized wind-down routine based on what's worked for you before.
02
Smart Fade
Once you drift off, MyDriftLAB detects the transition and silently switches from audio content to monitoring mode. No interruption. No manual stop.
03
Monitor All Night
On-device AI classifies every sound, tracks temperature, humidity, and light changes. Adaptive sounds adjust volume if noise spikes. All processing on your phone.
04
Morning Report
Wake up to a full breakdown: what disrupted you, when, how loud, and what to do about it. Patterns detected. Experiments suggested. Answers, not just numbers.

Stay Informed

The lab report

Everything happening in your bedroom while you sleep. Body sounds, environmental disruptions, air quality. All classified, timestamped, and decoded.

🎙
Noise Classification
On-device ML identifies specific sounds: traffic, dogs, sirens, HVAC, construction, neighbors. Each event logged with decibel level and exact timestamp.
😴
Snoring & Sleep Talk
Detects snoring, coughing, sleep talking, and breathing patterns. Distinguishes between you and your partner. Tracks duration and intensity.
🌡
Temperature Tracking
Ambient temperature monitored all night. Detects spikes from heating, cooling failures, or seasonal changes that fragment your sleep.
💧
Humidity Monitoring
Tracks humidity levels throughout the night. Alerts when you drop below 30% or spike above 60%. Correlates dry air with congestion and wake events.
🔆
Light Detection
Captures light intrusions from streetlights, devices, bathroom trips, and sunrise. Maps every light event to your disruption timeline.
📈
Pattern Detection
Weeks of data reveal recurring disruptions: Tuesday truck routes, weekend neighbors, seasonal temp shifts, humidity cycles. See patterns you'd never notice alone.
🔬
Sleep Experiments
MyDriftLAB suggests changes and tracks results. "Try earplugs tonight." "Set thermostat to 67F." "Add a humidifier." Measurable before-and-after data.
🧠
AI Sleep Coach
Personalized coaching based on YOUR data. Not generic tips. Real recommendations from your patterns, experiments, and environment history.
🔒
Privacy Sacred
All audio processing on-device. Raw audio never leaves your phone. Only event metadata stored. No cloud audio. We can't hear you. Period.
