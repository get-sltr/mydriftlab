3. Description of Service

MyDriftLAB is a sleep wellness application that provides:

(a) Sleep content including soundscapes, sleep stories, guided meditations, breathing exercises, and music designed to help users fall asleep;

(b) Sleep environment monitoring using your device's built-in microphone, light sensor (Android only), accelerometer, and barometer to detect ambient noise levels, noise events, light changes, and movement during sleep;

(c) Optional integration with Apple HealthKit to read health data from Apple Watch or other connected devices, including but not limited to heart rate, blood oxygen, sleep stages, respiratory rate, skin temperature, and heart rate variability;

(d) Morning reports summarizing sleep environment events and, where available, correlating environment data with body metrics;

(e) A Rest Score calculated from environment events and, where available, biometric data;

(f) Trend analysis and pattern detection across multiple nights of data;

(g) Sleep experiments ("Labs") allowing users to test environmental changes and measure their impact on sleep quality.

4. Audio Recording and Monitoring

4.1 On-Device Processing. MyDriftLAB uses your device's microphone to measure ambient noise levels (decibel readings) and detect noise events during sleep monitoring sessions. All audio processing occurs entirely on your device. Raw audio is never recorded, stored, transmitted, or uploaded to any server. The App extracts only numeric data (timestamps, decibel levels, duration, and event classification) from the audio stream.

4.2 No Audio Storage. MyDriftLAB does not retain, save, or have access to any audio recordings. A temporary audio file may be created by the operating system during monitoring, but it is neither read nor referenced by the App. Only numeric metadata derived from audio metering is used and stored.

4.3 Microphone Permission. You must grant microphone permission to use the sleep monitoring feature. You may revoke this permission at any time through your device settings, which will disable the monitoring functionality.

4.4 Consent. By activating the sleep monitoring feature, you expressly consent to the use of your device's microphone for the sole purpose of ambient noise level measurement and event detection as described in these Terms and our Privacy Policy.
